Frequently asked questions


How many people are permitted to stay in the apartment?

The apartment is ideal for two people, but it can comfortably accommodate up to four guests.


What are check in and check out times?

Check in after 03:00 pm. Checkout before 11:00 am.


Up to what time can I check in, what is I arrive late at night or early in the morning?

The building has a 24-hour reception, so you're welcome any time after 03:00pm.


What is the best way to get to the accommodation from the airport?

The easiest and most comfortable way is to take a taxi or an Uber from the airport.


Do you accept bookings outside of Airbnb?

All our bookings have to be made through Airbnb.


Can I pay some other way other than with credit card?

The Airbnb website provides up to date details about which mode of payment is acceptable.


Are children allowed?

Yes, children are welcome. The balcony is surrounded by a safety net for protection.


Is parking available?

Yes, parking is available onsite in the underground garage, please let us know so we can arrange access for you upon your arrival.


Are parties, concerts and other events allowed?

Parties, concerts and other similar events are not allowed.


Are pets allowed?

Pets are not allowed, with the exception of disability support animals.


Is smoking permitted?

It is not permitted inside the apartment. However, it is permitted to smoke on the balcony provided the windows and doors are closed. It is forbidden to throw the cigarettes butts off the balcony.


Do you offer beach chairs and umbrellas?

Yes, upon request they can be arranged.


Are towels and bed sheet included?

Yes, linen and towels are provided for use within the apartment. Beach towels however are not provided.
